Who Are We?

Green Kahuna

"The best surfer out there is the one having the most fun."

Our name Green Kahuna is a mixture of opposites, green coming from something new or fresh and Kahuna a Hawaiian word meanning wise man. We want to help everyone from whatever level or experience they have in surfing to make sure they get best out of the waves they ride. We're based in Malibu, California and we ship nationwide.
